### Summary

As the propulsion subteam lead, I was responsible for selecting the electronic components that would go into the plane. However, there is a notable difference between calculated performance and actual performance, so I created a testing rig to measure the static thrust of our battery, motor, and propeller combinations. Using the data gathered from this testing rig, I compared it to our calculated forces to determine if the components are were functioning as intended, or if something else needed to be done. Currently, we are working to integrate a shunt resistor into our circuit. This will aid us in finding the voltage and current running through the system, allowing us to determine if the motor is reaching its maximum rpm.
