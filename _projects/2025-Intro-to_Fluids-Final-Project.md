For my Fluids final project, my group was tasked with dissecting a hydraulic power unit used for wheelchair lift and explaining the fluid mechanics and concepts that went into it. Our analysis went over the way the machine functioned, assumptions we had made (mean flow, etc.), and mechanics (gear pump pressure differences, couette flow, etc.).
